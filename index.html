How to play the game:

You beat the computer if your choice "beats" his in the following way:
- Rock beats Scissors
- Scissors beats Paper
- Paper beats Rock
Each round you score 1 point for beating the opponent but you loose a point when the opponent beats you. To win the game you need to beat 5 times in a row, otherwise you loose.
